Ashwin Jacob

About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science and Engineering department of National Institute of Technology Calicut.

Prior to joining NIT Calicut, I was a Post Doctoral Researcher at the Ben Gurion University of the Negev, Israel (2022-2023), working with Meirav Zehavi.

I completed my Ph.D. from IMSc Chennai (2016-2022) advised by Venkatesh Raman.

Research Interests

Parameterized Algorithms, Kernelization, Graph Algorithms, Approximation Algorithms.
