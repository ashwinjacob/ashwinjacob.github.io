Workshop Overview

The AGATHA workshop is a national event focused on the latest developments in graph algorithms, hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at NIT Calicut. This workshop brings together leading researchers and students to discuss theoretical advancements and practical applications in graph algorithms.

The workshop will feature topics such as parameterized, randomized, and approximation algorithms, as well as subgraph detection and graph decomposition methods.
